Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tours

How do I book a tour?
You can book by calling us at 1 (902) 402-7263, emailing info@safiseasidetours.ca, using WhatsApp, or filling out our contact form. We'll confirm availability and arrange all details with you promptly.
What is your cancellation policy?
We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled tour. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee. Weather-related cancellations are handled on a case-by-case basis with full flexibility.
Are meals included in the tours?
Yes! All tours include complimentary Nova Scotia Lobster Rolls (or alternative options for dietary restrictions). Full-day tours may include additional meals. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when booking.
What time are pickup times?
Pickup times are flexible based on your preference and tour selection. Most tours begin between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM. We'll coordinate the exact time that works best for you when you book.
How many people can join a tour?
We specialize in private and small-group tours. Our comfortable vehicles can accommodate up to 6 passengers. For larger groups, please contact us for special arrangements and group rates.
What should I bring on the tour?
We recommend comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing (layers are best), sunscreen, and a camera. Water and snacks are provided in the vehicle. Don't forget your sense of adventure!
Are the tours suitable for children and seniors?
Absolutely! Our tours are designed to be comfortable and accessible for all ages. We can adjust the pace and activities based on your group's needs. Child seats are available upon request.
Do you offer tours in other languages?
Yes! Our guide, Asif Safi, is multilingual with over 15 years of international tourism experience. Please inquire about specific language requirements when booking your tour.
What happens if the weather is bad?
Nova Scotia weather can be unpredictable! We monitor conditions closely and will contact you if any changes are needed. We can reschedule or adjust the itinerary as necessary to ensure your safety and enjoyment.
